Dr. R.D. Sharma

Dr. R.D. Sharma is a former professor and head of Department of Sciences and Humanities in Delhi Skill & Enterpreneurship University (DESU), Delhi, India. Also, with Ph.D in Mathematics, he is a double gold medalist, ranking 1st in the order of merit in both B.Sc (Hons.) and M.Sc Examinations from the University of Rajasthan, Jaipur, India. He has undergone rigorious training from IIT Kharagpur in computer-oriented mathematical methods. He has more than 43 years of teaching experience to post-graduate, graduate and engineering students.
